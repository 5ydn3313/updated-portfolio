During my eight months at the Center For Book Arts (CBA), I was able to grow as a designer and printer. I designed & Risograph printed multiple promotional materials including bookmarks and the Spring Course Catalogue. Additionally, I designed and printed the Fall Events Zine with a promotional crossword inside highlighting upcoming workshops and skills potential members could learn at the center.

As an extension of this project I designed and developed an interactive answer key for the center website!
